Key Features

  • Simple Controls: Easy-to-learn swipe or tilt controls for players of all ages.
  • Challenging Gameplay: Catch the right items while avoiding the wrong ones to win.
  • Time-Limited Fun: Race against the clock with only 40 seconds to achieve your goal.
  • Dynamic Scoring: Collect fruits to increase your score but beware—catching a bomb will cost you valuable points.
  • Victory Condition: Score 25 fruits within the time limit to win.
  • Bright & Engaging Graphics: Vibrant visuals and smooth animations create a captivating experience.

Gameplay Description

In BasketCatch, your task is simple yet addictive: fruits are falling from the sky, and you must catch them in your basket. Sounds easy? Here’s the twist—mixed among the fruits are bombs, and catching one will make you lose points!

With only 40 seconds on the clock, your goal is to collect 25 fruits to win. Use quick reflexes to maneuver your basket left and right, grabbing the delicious fruits while skillfully avoiding the dangerous bombs.

Do you have what it takes to master the art of BasketCatch and secure the ultimate high score?
